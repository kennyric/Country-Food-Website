EQUATORIAL GUINEA FOOD




Equatorial Guinea has one of the richest gastronomy, and it is very notable in its diversity. The food in Equatorial Guinea range from the native to western food. The gastronomy of Equatorial Guinea is a mixture of cuisines from the native tribes, Spain (its colonial homeland) and the Islamic States such as Morocco. As the richest nation in West Africa, its cuisine incorporates several types of meat. Fish and chicken are common dishes. Chilies and other spices are also very popular. Equatorial Guinea has never been a particularly powerful nation, so its cuisine has been influenced by other nations for centuries.
